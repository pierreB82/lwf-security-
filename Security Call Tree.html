    📋 Purpose

    The communication tree enables rapid top-down dissemination of critical information to all relevant staff, and confirms that each person has received and understood the message.

    Information flows downward in a cascade. This is not a bidirectional communication tool.
    🔑 Activation authority
    Activation is restricted to officially designated persons. Not every staff member can decide to activate the tree.

      Identify the designated activators for your context and ensure their alternates are also named and known.

      ⚙ How it works

      The system works through cascade transmission. Each person receives a message and forwards it immediately to their assigned contacts in the tree.

      Each staff member only needs to know: who will contact them, who they must contact, and who to alert if they face difficulties.
      Design rule: each person must have a maximum of 2 direct contacts in the tree. More than 2 makes transmission difficult to manage in an emergency.

      Confirmations of receipt flow back up progressively to the person who activated the tree, creating a confirmation loop.

      Structure: the national tree (integrated in the Country Security Plan) activates local trees (each integrated in the Local Security Pack). The national tree connects country leadership to office managers; each local tree covers all office staff.
      📱 Communication means
        Email is not a suitable means for activating a communication tree.
        A WhatsApp group does not replace the tree: it creates no chain of responsibility, no escalation, and does not work without internet.
        ✓ Confirmation and unreachable persons
        Always confirm: receipt, understanding, and transmission to your contacts. A simple reply is enough: "Received", "Understood", "OK".

        If someone is unreachable, do not stop the cascade. Make several attempts, then report the gap to the SFP or office manager. Never let an entire branch be blocked by a single unreachable person.

        Closing an activation: each branch lead confirms upward that their contacts have been reached or identified as unreachable. Confirmations flow up until they reach the activator. The activation is closed only when the full loop is complete.
        🔄 Tree maintenance
          Regular testing required - minimum every 6 months, after any significant staff change, after reorganisation, or after changes in communication means. Evaluate: response rate, dissemination speed, contact accuracy, and resilience when a member is unavailable.
